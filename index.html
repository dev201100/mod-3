Professor

The Professor is the leader of the gang, and the one who carefully plans and organises the assault on the Royal Mint and the Bank of Spain in Madrid. With a remarkable culture, but with a nerdy appearance and components. The idea of the first heist is to avenge his father. His grandfather had fought against the fascists in Italy.
Good thing

  • Brain of the heist
  • Controlling and Management
Bad thing
  • Mess things up

Lisbon

Lisbon aka Raquel Murillo is an inspector of the National Police Corps tasked with negotiating with the robbers (seasons 1 and 2). However, during the negotiations, she met the Professor, She falls in love with him to the point of choosing to follow him instead of arresting him and at that point she becomes an effective member of the gang.
Good thing

  • Strong woman
  • Sensitive
Bad thing
  • Easy to mainpulate

Helsinki

Helsinki is the tallest out of the robbers, and possibly the heaviest too. He has a large frame and is very strong, like his cousin Oslo, presumably due to their backgrounds in war. He is completely bald on his scalp and sports a large beard. He is a robber and war veteran who participated in both the Royal Mint of Spain and Bank of Spain heists.
